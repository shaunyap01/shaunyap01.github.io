About Me

Hey there! I'm Shaun, a data enthusiast with a love for numbers, patterns, and all things analytical. Ever since I was young, I've been drawn to the beauty of mathematics, competing in math competitions and diving deep into the world of statistics and machine learning.

With over five years of experience in statistics and machine learning, I bring a strong foundation in data-driven problem-solving. I love uncovering insights from complex datasets, building models, and using data to tell compelling stories.

Outside of data science, I’m a huge sports fan. I used to play competitive golf and even represented KL (the capital of Malaysia) in competitions. While golf was my main sport, I’ll pretty much play anything—badminton, climbing—you name it!

I’m always up for a good chat, whether it’s about data science, sports, or just sharing interesting ideas. Feel free to reach out—I'd love to connect!
