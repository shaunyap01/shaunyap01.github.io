About me

I’m a PhD researcher at University College London (UCL) part of the Tech4Health Centre for Doctoral Training (Tech4Health CDT). Supported by a 4-year studentship co-funded by UKRI Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC) and The National Brain Appeal, my research sits at the intersection of neuroscience and data science - focusing on visual system vulnerability in dementia.
